\re{Carrot Bread}
{\fr{3}{4} Cup Sugar \\
 1 Cup Flour \\
 \fr{1}{2} Cup Whole Wheat Flour \\
 1 Teaspoon Baking Powder \\
 1 Teaspoon Baking Soda \\
 1 Teaspoon Salt \\
 1 Cinnamon \\
 \fr{3}{4} Cup Applesauce \\
 1 Egg \\
 1 Teaspoon Vanilla \\
 1 Cup Shredded Carrot \\
 \fr{1}{4} Cup Chopped Pecans}
{Mix wet ingredients, mix dry ingredients. Mix together and then add carrots and pecans. Bake at 350\degree for 20 minutes for muffins, 50-55 minutes for bread.}
