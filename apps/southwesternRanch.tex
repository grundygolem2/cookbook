\re{Southwestern Ranch Dip}
{16 Ounces light sour cream \\
 2 Cups Mayonnaise or Light Mayo \\
 \fr{1}{2} Teaspoon Ground Red Chipotle Pepper (Or 1 Teaspoon Chili Powder and a Pinch of Cayenne)\\
 1 Teaspoon Ground Cumin \\
 2 Teaspoons Taco Seasoning \\
 1 Package Ranch Dressing Mix \\
 1 Can gren Chilis, Drained}
{Combine and Chill before Serving}
